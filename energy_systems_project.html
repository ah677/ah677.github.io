Consider multi-interval economic dispatch where the dispatch of all intervals are determined simultaneously. Let P_it be the generation level of generator i in interval t. The multi-interval economic dispatch is to determine the optimal dispatch level P*it for all generators and the flexible demand D*4t in all intervals, subject to (i) the power balance constraints in each interval and (ii) ramping constraint between adjacent intervals.
